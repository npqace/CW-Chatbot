The VSTEP Chatbot is a bilingual assistant designed to help users with inquiries about the VSTEP exam, including its structure, scoring, fees, and more. Whether you're looking for detailed exam guidelines or simple FAQs, the chatbot provides instant responses in both English and Vietnamese to ensure a seamless user experience.

This chatbot is powered by advanced technologies such as:

  • Flask, which serves as the backbone of the web application.
  • RAG (Retrieve-Augmented Generation), a sophisticated technique that handles user queries by first retrieving relevant information from a dataset and then generating answers using a large language model (LLM).
  • Gemini API, integrated to enhance the chatbot's performance and accuracy.

With these technologies, the VSTEP Chatbot delivers reliable and precise information to support users in preparing for the exam.
